About LIC Pension Plus

LIC Pension Plus is a unit-linked pension plan offered by Life Insurance Corporation of India. It is designed to provide financial security to individuals during their retirement years.

Features

  • Flexibility to choose the premium payment term
  • Investment options in equity, debt or a combination of both
  • Partial withdrawal option after 5 years
  • Life cover option for added protection
  • Tax benefits under section 80C and 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act

Benefits

  • Regular income after retirement
  • Option to receive the benefit amount in a lump sum
  • Flexibility to choose the annuity option
  • Life cover to ensure financial security for loved ones
  • Option to switch between funds to manage risk

FAQ

What is the minimum age to buy LIC Pension Plus?

The minimum age to buy LIC Pension Plus is 18 years.

What is the maximum age to buy LIC Pension Plus?

The maximum age to buy LIC Pension Plus is 65 years.

Is there a limit on the amount that can be invested in LIC Pension Plus?

Yes, the minimum investment amount is Rs. 1,000 per month or Rs. 10,000 annually, and there is no maximum limit.
